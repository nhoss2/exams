In a METAR for YAAA, the figures quoted immediately before the QNH read 9/8, and at YBBB they read 10/8. This means that, at place YAAA, the dry bulb is 9C and the:

  1. Dew point is 8C and fog is closer to forming at YAAA than YBBB

  2. Wet bulb is 8C and fog is closer to forming at YAAA than YBBB

  3. Dew point is 8C and fog is closer to forming at YBBB than YAAA

  4. Wet bulb is 8C and fog is closer to forming at YBBB than YAAA
