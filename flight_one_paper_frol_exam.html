If you switch to another ATC unit, wait to find no other transmissions and make your call to find the response: TWO STATIONS IN TOGETHER ON BRISBANE CENTRE. This means:

  1. Your transmission was over transmitted by someone else and you should await their call then make yours

  2. Your transmission was over transmitted by someone else and you should make your call again quickly before the jump on your transmission a second time
